Types of Energy

☀️

Solar Energy

Energy harnessed from the sun's radiation.

🌬️

Wind Energy

Power generated by wind turbines.

💧

Hydroelectric Energy

Electricity produced from flowing water.

🌱

Biomass Energy

Energy obtained from organic materials.

🔥

Geothermal Energy

Heat energy extracted from the Earth's interior.

⚛️

Nuclear Energy

Energy released from atomic nuclei through fission or fusion.

🔋

Chemical Energy

Energy stored in chemical bonds, released through reactions.

🏋️

Kinetic Energy

Energy of motion possessed by moving objects.

⚡

Electrical Energy

Energy from electric charges or current flow.

🎇

Radiant Energy

Energy carried by electromagnetic waves, such as light.

🔊

Sound Energy

Energy produced by vibrating objects, transmitted as sound waves.

🧲

Magnetic Energy

Energy stored within magnetic fields.
